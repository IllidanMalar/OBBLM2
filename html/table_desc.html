Description of table columns
  • "*" marks a group average (ie. the group devided by the number of played matches).
  • When sorting against a column "-" denotes descending and "+" ascending.
  • W, L and D denote Won, Lost and Draw.
  • SW, SL and SD denote the largest Win, Lose and Draw Streaks.
  • GP is Games Played.
  • WIN% is the Win Percentage.
  • GF and GA are Goals For and Against.
  • WT is Won Tournaments.
  • PTS is Points
  • SMP denotes Sportsmanship Points
  • tcasf and tcasa denotes total team cas For and Against
  • sdiff denotes score difference (ie. arithmetic value of GF-GA)
  • tcdiff denotes total team cas difference (ie. arithmetic value of tcasf-tcasa)
